Strategy
Step 3 — Derive allocation approach from goal + market
Not Run
Strategy Rationale
  • Strategy is derived by combining your financial goal (Step 1) with the current market regime (Step 2).
  • RITA's Double DQN model is trained on Nifty 50 OHLCV data with 9 features including EMAs, RSI, MACD, BB, ATR and Trend Score.
  • Regime detection uses EMA-26/EMA-50 ratio — ratio < 0.99 for 3+ consecutive days signals a Bear regime; otherwise Bull.
  • Allocation adjusts equity exposure based on regime, feasibility of your target return, and risk tolerance.
  • Constraints: Sharpe Ratio ≥ 1.0 · Max Drawdown ≤ 10%.
Click "Design Strategy" to generate an allocation recommendation.
